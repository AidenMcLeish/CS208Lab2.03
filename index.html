Montpelier, Idaho, USA

Location

Montpelier is located in the Southeastern corner of Idaho, just north of the great Bear Lake.

About

Weather

The warm summers bring many tourists to Bear Lake, but the harsh winters always prevent them from staying too long.

Population

Montpelier is a very small town, only sporting about 3,000 people.

Sports

Girls sports have always performed well in the area, particularly volleyball. Locals enjoy watching football, but our teams have never been very good.

Things To Do

  • Boating
  • Fishing
  • Snowboarding
